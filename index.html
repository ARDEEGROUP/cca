CCA by The Ardee Foundation, a first-of-its-kind space for culture, thought and art.

A space where guests and collectors engage with the ever changing works on the walls of the living rooms, the library offers space for quiet reflection, research and conversation, the sculpture gardens become a haven for art lovers and a gallery showcases the work done at the various artist studios at the annexe paving the way for the future masters of India just as CCA did in the nineties.
A vibrant cultural annual programme with senior artists, curators, art historians, museum directors and performances will keep bringing guests back for more.
